Text, email, Messenger… any way you like! Just copy and paste the unique link you’re given.

Can I post my iDecide instead of sending it?

Yes! Using the Post A Link feature in your back office, you can post generic iDecide presentations on websites, in emails, auto responders, on social media… anywhere!

Is there a limit on how many presentations I can send?

No! Use iDecide as often as you’d like and as much as you’d like!

What happens to my presentations if I cancel my account?

Your presentations and any links you have sent out will be deactivated upon cancellation.

How do I edit or delete contacts?

Hover over the name and click the edit icon to the far right. Depending on your device, you may have to scroll down to see the DELETE CONTACT button.

Downloading the free iDecide App is the easiest way to text presentations to your contacts. Simply download the app and login using your existing account..

What if my payment won’t go through?
